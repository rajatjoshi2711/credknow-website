Our Key Products

PRAMO

Document Intelligence Platform

An AI-powered document intelligence platform that helps enterprises process large-scale documents with unmatched accuracy and speed.

  • AI/ML Extraction & OCR for multi-format, multilingual documents
  • Smart, compliance-ready automation workflows with human-in-the-loop review
  • Real-time analytics and monitoring and validations
  • Compatibility with Tally, Xero, Zoho, etc.

CredKnowMoney

Loan Lifecycle Management

A comprehensive loan management system that automates the entire loan lifecycle from onboarding to repayment, empowering lenders with efficiency.

  • Digital onboarding with integrated e-KYC and document management
  • Automated credit scoring for smarter applicant evaluation and faster approvals
  • Centralized and scalable cloud-based loan management platform
  • Compliance-ready workflows

CrediKnow

AI-based Credit Assessment

Leverages AI-powered scoring models to help banks and NBFCs evaluate borrower credibility and risk with confidence and speed

  • Automated creditworthiness & risk profiling
  • Multi-source integration from credit bureaus
  • Built-in KYC/AML regulatory compliance, real-time alerts

PRAMOInsure

Insurance Processing, Assessment & Audit

Designed to help insurers, TPAs, and corporates achieve compliance, efficiency, and transparency while minimizing fraud and operational risks

  • AI-Powered audits for claims, underwriting and policy compliance.
  • Fraud detection, risk control, and anomaly identification
  • Smart Claims Processing with automated validation and pre-authorization
  • End-to-end insurance lifecycle support - from claims, audit and policy review to digital processing and regulatory compliance
